lorenz

I learnt about this strange attractor from Daniel Shiffman and based my research and code off of his video. E. N. Lorenz discovered this 3-dimensional differential equation by simplifying a weather model. The behaviour it exhibits is called "deterministic chaos" (www.math.toronto.edu, n.d.). It's one of the earliest discoveries of the butterfly effect because of the hugely differentiating results based on tiny changes in the initial values used.

I started out in Processing to be more comfortable and then moved to JavaScript once I felt I knew how the system was working. It's a simple shape but reminds me of a fairytale vine.
